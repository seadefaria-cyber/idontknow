## Tone Guide per Archetype

| Archetype | Tone | Example |
|-----------|------|---------|
| Underground Discovery | Excited, authentic, "I just found gold" | "OK so I was deep in SoundCloud at 3am..." |
| Rap News | Professional but accessible, informative | "This week saw major moves in hip-hop..." |
| Hot Takes | Bold, unapologetic, debate-starting | "I don't care what anyone says..." |
| Snippets | Minimal words, let the music speak | "Just listen. Full volume." |
| Rap Culture | Relatable, funny, community-building | "If you know, you know..." |
