Overview

DeFaria NYC is a content agency specializing in short-form viral media for recording artists and labels. We build and manage fan pages, produce clips from artist footage, create slideshow content, and distribute across TikTok, Instagram, X, and YouTube Shorts. Our team handles every step — from moment selection and captioning to posting strategy and performance analysis.

Goal: Reach $5,000/month in recurring client revenue by Month 3 (May 2026), fully covering all business and personal expenses with profit.

Who Each Tier Is For

  • Starter ($2,000/mo) — Independent artists and small managers testing fan page growth. Low-risk entry point with real results in 30–60 days.
  • Growth ($3,500/mo) — Artists with label support or managers with 2–3 artists who want full cross-platform coverage and strategic input.
  • Pro ($5,000/mo) — Labels and established artists who want a full content team. Clips + slideshows + trend-reactive posts + weekly strategy. Equivalent to hiring a full-time social media person at a fraction of the cost.

Paying Yourself — Owner's Draw

As a single-member LLC, you pay yourself via owner's draw — a direct transfer from the business bank account to your personal account. No payroll required.

AllocationRuleExample ($10K revenue, $1,500 expenses)
Taxes (reserve)Set aside 30%$2,550/mo into savings
Business reserveKeep 15% in business account$1,275/mo
Your draw (paycheck)Take the remaining 55%$4,675/mo
Quarterly tax payments (mandatory if you owe $1,000+/year): April 15, June 16, September 15, January 15. Set aside 30% of every payment received into a separate savings account.

S Corp election: Once the LLC nets $60,000–$80,000+/year, consider filing Form 2553 to elect S Corp status. This lets you pay yourself a reasonable salary and take the rest as distributions, saving $5,000–$10,000/year in self-employment tax.
