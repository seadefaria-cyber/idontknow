Overview

DeFaria NYC is a clipping and fan page agency specializing in underground rap artists. We create, distribute, and manage viral short-form content across TikTok, Instagram, and X using a combination of AI automation (Claude Code, Larry/OpenClaw) and human editorial oversight.

Goal: Reach $3,000+/month in recurring client revenue by Month 3, covering rent ($2,000) and all business expenses ($1,280) with profit.
