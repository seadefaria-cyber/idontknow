How The System Works

From first conversation to full-scale viral distribution — the machine runs itself.

Meet

Tell us about your artist and your goals. We assess the content and build a strategy.

Study

Our editors analyze every frame of your footage. We identify exactly what will trigger the algorithm.

Plan

We build the seeding map. Which accounts, which platforms, which timing — every variable is controlled.

Execute

The system goes live. Clips deploy, seeds activate, and your content saturates every feed. We force it.

2

How We Force It

Consulting

We map out the entire plan of attack for the month. How many clips go out per day, which platforms to hit, what content to prioritize. We assemble the team of editors and build the full strategy before anything goes live.

Viral Clipping

We dissect every frame of your content to extract the exact moments that force engagement. Hooks, pacing, captions, cuts — every clip is precision-engineered to stop the scroll and trigger the algorithm.

Distribution

Every clip gets micro-seeded across TikTok, Reels, Shorts, Twitch, X, and Kik simultaneously through our network of fan pages, niche communities, and seeding accounts. Hundreds of placements, all coordinated to saturate every feed.

Botting

We deploy engagement bots to inflate your numbers — likes, comments, views, shares. All coordinated to trigger the algorithm and push your content past the tipping point. Real momentum, manufactured at scale.
