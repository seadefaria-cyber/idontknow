What We Do

Seeding & Growth

Micro-Seeding

We plant your content across the internet so strategically it looks organic. Fan pages, niche accounts, curated placements — untraceable back to your artist.

Viral Clipping

Our editors identify the highest-impact moments from your long-form and cut them into scroll-stopping clips. Every clip is hand-selected.

Captions & Hooks

Word-synced captions and engineered text hooks on every clip. Designed to stop the scroll and drive watch time.

Fan Page Network

We build and manage a network of fan pages for your artist — multiplying your reach across every platform's algorithm.

Multi-Platform

TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, X — your content goes where your audience scrolls. Scheduled and managed.

Growth Analytics

Real-time performance tracking across every account and platform. We show you what's working and double down on it.

Full-Service Creative

Beyond Clipping

From music videos to commercials, we handle every step. Led by our founder — a filmmaker, scorer, and Twitch partner — every project gets a director's eye.

Directing

Music videos, content shoots, and short films — directed with vision and purpose.

Scoring

Original scoring and sound design for commercials, videos, and trailers.

Post-Production

Color grading, VFX, and finishing for music videos, visualizers, and branded content.

Video Editing

Full editorial for commercials, promos, and long-form content. Clean cuts, sharp pacing.

Live Production

Live streaming production for events, concerts, and broadcasts. Twitch partner.

Media Seeding

Strategic placement across publications and media outlets. We get your artist covered.
